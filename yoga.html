What does yoga do for you?

Practicing yoga helps you to be more calm Slow breathing, relaxation, gentle movements, and meditation help you focus deeply on your mind, observe every change in your body, understand your body, and thereby improve your health.

Why is yoga usefull ?

Yoga is not the usual bending movements and postures as many people think. Yoga is a combination of physical and mental practices aimed at the unification of mind, body and spirit. The aim of Yoga is to achieve inner equilibrium and come to a state of 'enlightenment'
